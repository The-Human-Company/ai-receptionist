# Email Templates

## Single Email Templates

### Template 1: The Value-First Email

```
Subject: [CURIOSITY HOOK - benefit or question]
Preview: [EXPANDS ON SUBJECT]

Hey [NAME],

[RELATABLE OPENER - 1-2 sentences connecting to their world]

[STORY OR OBSERVATION that sets up the problem]

Here's what most people don't realize:

[INSIGHT #1]

[INSIGHT #2]

[INSIGHT #3]

The people who understand this [POSITIVE OUTCOME].

The ones who don't? [NEGATIVE OUTCOME]

That's exactly why I [CREATED/BUILT/PUT TOGETHER] [PRODUCT] - [ONE-LINE VALUE PROP].

[CTA BUTTON: Clear action text]

[SIGN OFF],
[NAME]

P.S. - [SECONDARY HOOK or urgency element]
```

## Email Sequence Templates

### 5-Email Nurture Sequence

**Email 1: The Welcome / Origin Story**
```
Subject: Welcome (and a quick story)
Preview: Why I started [PRODUCT/COMPANY]

Hey [NAME],

Thanks for [JOINING/SIGNING UP/DOWNLOADING].

Before I send you [WHAT THEY SIGNED UP FOR], I want to share why I built this.

[ORIGIN STORY - 3-4 paragraphs]
- The problem you faced
- Why existing solutions didn't work
- The moment you decided to build something different
- What drives you now

[PRODUCT] isn't just another [CATEGORY]. It's [YOUR UNIQUE APPROACH].

Over the next few days, I'll share:
• [WHAT EMAIL 2 COVERS]
• [WHAT EMAIL 3 COVERS]
• [WHAT EMAIL 4 COVERS]

For now, [SIMPLE ASK - reply, bookmark, etc.]

Talk soon,
[NAME]
```

## Subject Line Swipe File

**Curiosity:**
- "The one thing I'd do differently..."
- "I was wrong about [TOPIC]"
- "This changed everything"
- "Stop doing this immediately"

**Benefit-Driven:**
- "How to [RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]"
- "[NUMBER] [RESULTS] without [PAIN]"
- "The fastest way to [OUTCOME]"

**Story-Based:**
- "[NAME]'s story: [INTRIGUING DETAIL]"
- "What happened when I [DID THING]"
- "I almost [GAVE UP/FAILED]"

**Personal:**
- "Quick question for you"
- "Can I be honest?"
- "I need to tell you something"

**FOMO:**
- "[TIMEFRAME] left"
- "Last chance: [OFFER]"
- "I'm closing this tomorrow"
